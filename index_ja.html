研究紹介

I am a Ph.D. candidate at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Dr. Cheng-Ku Yu. My research focuses on orographic precipitation and extreme rainfall associated with tropical cyclones in Taiwan and southern Japan. I use polarimetric radar observations, numerical simulations, and field campaign data to investigate precipitation microphysics and dynamics. I am currently seeking a postdoctoral position starting in 2026.
