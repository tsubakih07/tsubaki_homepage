About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Prof. Cheng-Ku Yu in the Mesoscale and Orographic Precipitation Laboratory. I was born and raised in Okinawa, Japan.

My research focuses on orographic precipitation and extreme rainfall in Taiwan associated with tropical cyclones. I use polarimetric Doppler radar observations, rain gauges, and Parsivel disdrometers to investigate precipitation microphysics and dynamics.
