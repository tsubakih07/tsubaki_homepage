About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at National Taiwan University under the supervision of Dr. Cheng-Ku Yu. My research focuses on orographic precipitation and extreme rainfall associated with tropical cyclones in Taiwan. I use polarimetric Doppler radar observations, rain gauges, and Parsivel disdrometers to investigate precipitation microphysics and dynamics. I am originally from Okinawa, Japan.
