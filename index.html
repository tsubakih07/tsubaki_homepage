About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at National Taiwan University, working with Prof. Cheng-Ku Yu in the Mesoscale and Orographic Precipitation Laboratory.

My research examines orographic precipitation and extreme rainfall from tropical cyclones in Taiwan using polarimetric Doppler radar, rain gauges, and disdrometers.

Growing up in Okinawa, Japan, where typhoons are a familiar part of life, inspired me to study tropical cyclone rainfall and its underlying processes.
